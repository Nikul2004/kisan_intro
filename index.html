My Ambitions and Objectives

To break the chain of the Kishan Business industry and make it profitable, the first step would be to make the industry more efficient. This can be achieved by implementing modern technology and processes that streamline operations and reduce wastage.

Additionally, focusing on improving the quality of products and services offered by Kishan businesses can help attract more customers and increase profitability. This can be done by providing proper training and education to the workers, ensuring that they have the necessary skills to produce high-quality goods.

Breaking the brokers market in every Kishan industry is crucial to increase profitability. This can be done by establishing direct relationships with customers and bypassing brokers. Offering competitive prices, attractive incentives, and excellent customer service can help attract customers and break their dependency on brokers.

By implementing these strategies, the Kishan Business industry can become more profitable and efficient, breaking the chains of traditional practices and embracing modern techniques for growth and success.
